Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know Before Your Visit

Here are answers to some of the questions our clients ask most often.

Do I need to book an appointment?

Yes. We recommend booking in advance to reserve your preferred date and time.

Do you accept walk-in clients?

Walk-ins are welcome when we have availability, but appointments are always recommended.

Do you accept M-Pesa payments?

Yes. We accept M-Pesa as well as other convenient payment methods.

How long do knotless braids take?

The time depends on the style and length, but most appointments take between 3 and 6 hours.

How can I book?

Simply click the WhatsApp button on this website and send us a message. We'll help you schedule your appointment.
